About me

I’m Andrias Barmelki, a 39-year-old web developer from Sweden with a deep passion for technology and problem-solving. My journey into tech started with a love for computers in my early years. After working on cruise ships for over a decade, I made the decision to shift careers and pursue my passion full-time.

I hold a degree in Object-Oriented Programming with AI/ML from NBI Handelsakademin, which I completed in the summer of 2024. Throughout the years, I've developed a good set of skills in programming, including Python, C#, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My portfolio includes projects that explore face-recognition models, hotel booking systems, movie database applications and more.

Outside of coding, I enjoy filming with my drone, creating content for my YouTube channel, hanging out with friends and watch football. I'm currently seeking new opportunities in web development and AI, and I'm always excited to collaborate on innovative projects.
